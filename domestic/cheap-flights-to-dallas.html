  • Sixth Floor Museum at Dealey Plaza :  Explore the incident of President John F. Kennedy's death in the sixth-floor museum of Dealey Plaza. History buffs can learn the complete story of the tragedy of President John F. Kennedy's murder here by catching their flights to Dallas. 
  • Dallas Museum of Art : Witness around 24,000 artworks at this museum. Moreover, you can see a variety of classical art and artifacts from archaic Egypt, Greece, and Rome here. Also, the site contains paintings and several modern pieces that are obtained from throughout the world.
  • Dallas Zoo : Take your flights to Dallas to meet with more than 2,000 unfamiliar animals at Dallas Zoo. You can spot around 406 species of animals here in various habitats. The zoo is divided into regional areas including ZooNorth, the Wilds of Africa, Giants of the Savanna, and a children's zoo that give you a chance to encounter unique animals.
